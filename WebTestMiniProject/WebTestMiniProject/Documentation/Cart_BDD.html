Cart_BDD

In order to be able to complete the order As a registered user of the saucedemo website I want to navigate the cart page

  • Tags: @Cart

    ContinueShoppingFromCart

    • Given I am on the cart page
    • When I press the continue shopping button
    • Then I should land on the "inventory" page

  • Tags: @Cart

    CheckoutNoItems

    • Given I am on the cart page
    • And I have no items in my cart
    • When I press the checkout button
    • Then I should land on the "checkout-step-one" page

  • Tags: @Cart

    CheckoutWithItems

    • Given I am on the cart page with items
    • And I have items in my cart
    • When I press the checkout button
    • Then I should land on the "checkout-step-one" page

  • Tags: @Cart

    RemoveItemFromCart

    • Given I am on the cart page with items
    • And I have items in my cart
    • When I press the remove button
